Young Womens Voices

A safe space for women and girls

A chance to share your views and experiences in a kind and supportive group of others who identify as female

Based in Heaton Norris, we provide young women aged 10 - 16 with a safe spcae to express themsevles, make new friends and socialise

WHO WE ARE

We are YOUNG WOMENS VOICES, a community group in Heaton Norris which aims to create a safe space for women. Our core values are respect, kindness and being supportive, and if that sounds like you we would love you to get involved!

WHAT WE DO

We are a safe space to talk, share and meet other like-minded young women and girls. We talk about the issues important to YOU, such as mental health and wellbeing, hate crimes, LGBTQ+ discussions and much much more. (If you want to raise a topic for one of our meetings, feel free to post on the Discussion Board!) We do videography, photography and other activities that aim to build confidence and teamwork. We meet every Tuesday at 4.30 at Heaton Norris Community Centre, with free food cooked each week by one of our regular members.
